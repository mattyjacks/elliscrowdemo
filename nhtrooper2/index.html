Our Mission

Serving Troopers, Protecting Communities

The New Hampshire Troopers Association supports our troopers while funding vital community programs that save lives and strengthen our state. Through generous donations from supporters like you, we provide drug education to prevent addiction, financial support for families of fallen heroes, scholarships for future law enforcement professionals, and critical funding for children's healthcare. Together, we're making New Hampshire safer and stronger for everyone.

  • Troop C (Keene): Cheshire & Sullivan Counties

Eastern Region

  • Troop A (Epping): Rockingham & Strafford Counties
