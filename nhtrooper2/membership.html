How to Join

Becoming a Member is Easy

If you're an active trooper or sergeant with the New Hampshire State Police, joining the association is straightforward. Membership is voluntary and provides you with a strong, unified voice in advocating for your rights, benefits, and professional welfare.

Step 1: Review Benefits

Familiarize yourself with the comprehensive benefits package available to members. Understanding what the association offers will help you make an informed decision about membership.

Step 2: Contact Us

Reach out to the association via phone, email, or in person at our Concord office. Our staff will provide you with membership information and answer any questions.

Step 3: Complete Application

Fill out the membership application form with your information. The process is simple and can typically be completed in just a few minutes.

Step 4: Start Enjoying Benefits

Once your application is processed, you'll immediately have access to all member benefits, resources, and support services offered by the association.
